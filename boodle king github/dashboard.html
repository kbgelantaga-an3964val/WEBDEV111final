What is Boodle Fight?

Boodle fight is a Filipino military style of eating where food is served on banana leaves laid out on a long table. Everyone eats together using their hands, creating a fun, communal dining experience that brings people closer together. It's not just a meal—it's a celebration of Filipino culture, hospitality, and the joy of sharing food with loved ones.
