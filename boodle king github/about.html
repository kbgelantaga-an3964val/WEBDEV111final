Our Story

Boodle King was born from a deep appreciation for Filipino culture and the unique way Filipinos celebrate togetherness. The boodle fight tradition, originally from military culture, has become a beloved Filipino dining experience that symbolizes equality, unity, and camaraderie.

We’ve perfected the art of preparing traditional Filipino dishes using recipes passed down through generations. From our perfectly grilled bangus to our savory pork BBQ skewers, every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail.
